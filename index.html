My Story

Hi, Im ABDO a self-taught Discord bot developer and the creator of Skirk. My interest in development started through online gaming and Discord communities, where I became curious about how games and systems bring people together.

Over time, that curiosity turned into building Skirk a multi-game Discord bot inspired by games like Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail, and Wuthering Waves, with a strong focus on progression, gacha systems, and community interaction.

Outside of development, I enjoy gaming, watching anime, and listening to music.
